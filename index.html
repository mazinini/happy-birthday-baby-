My Beloved

typing these thoughts fills me with excitement—I cannot wait for you to read them. but I must hold myself back and wait for your special day to arrive… and that day is today. I’ve said this countless times: you are my answered prayer, my other half, my soulmate. you mean so much to me. If only these small hands of mine could give you your heart’s deepest desire, I would do so without hesitation. maybe we truly are meant for each other. just imagine how our paths crossed—especially through “chismis” where ourr first interaction and that led me to you. and still, I can’t believe I actually have you.

maybe the red string of fate really does work, even in online world. we didn’t have the most charming first impression—or at least, that’s how I felt. I didn’t like your pa-cold personality at first... but maybe that’s what made everything even more memorable in the end. your quirks, character, and personality captivates me so much. hindi ko alam before na unti-unti na pala me nahuhulog saiyo, just imagine how you can get someone's attention that easily. kung pwede kandaduhan na kita para hindi maagaw, hehe joke.
